## Course 1: Feldenkrais Method Fundamentals

**Category**: Mental Health & Wellness  
**Difficulty**: Beginner  
**Estimated Time**: 12 hours  
**Modules**: 5  
**Lessons**: 20

### Overview
Learn the Feldenkrais Method to improve body awareness, reduce stress, and enhance emotional regulation through gentle movement practices. This course integrates somatic approaches with mental health awareness.

### Learning Objectives
- Understand the principles of the Feldenkrais Method
- Develop body awareness for stress reduction
- Practice gentle movements for emotional regulation
- Integrate somatic practices into daily life
- Recognize movement patterns that contribute to anxiety

### Course Structure

#### Module 1: Introduction to Feldenkrais (4 lessons, 75 min)
1. **What is the Feldenkrais Method?** (Video, 15 min)
   - History and principles of the method
   - Moshe Feldenkrais's discoveries
   - Applications for mental health

2. **The Mind-Body Connection** (Article, 20 min)
   - Scientific basis for somatic practices
   - How movement affects emotions
   - The role of awareness in healing

3. **Body Awareness Assessment** (Quiz, 10 min)
   - Self-assessment questions
   - Understanding current body awareness level

4. **Your First Awareness Through Movement Lesson** (Exercise, 30 min)
   - Guided audio exercise
   - Basic lying-down movements
   - Reflection prompts

#### Module 2: Body Awareness for Stress Relief (4 lessons, 68 min)
1. **Recognizing Tension Patterns** (Video, 18 min)
   - Common areas of tension
   - Mind-body patterns
   - Self-scanning techniques

2. **The Pelvic Clock Exercise** (Exercise, 25 min)
   - Step-by-step instructions
   - Audio guided practice
   - Benefits for lower back tension

3. **Breathing and Movement Integration** (Article, 15 min)
   - Conscious breathing techniques
   - Pairing breath with movement
   - Stress reduction mechanisms

4. **Stress Relief Practice Check** (Quiz, 10 min)
   - Knowledge verification
   - Practice reflection

#### Module 3: Movement Patterns for Emotional Regulation (4 lessons, 90 min)
1. **Emotions in the Body** (Video, 20 min)
   - Where emotions are stored
   - Somatic experiencing
   - Trauma-informed movement

2. **Gentle Rolling Movements** (Exercise, 30 min)
   - Side-to-side rolling
   - Releasing stored tension
   - Safety and modifications

3. **Recognizing Habitual Patterns** (Article, 25 min)
   - Identifying movement habits
   - Connection to emotional states
   - Creating new patterns

4. **Self-Compassion in Movement** (Video, 15 min)
   - Non-judgmental awareness
   - Working within comfort zones
   - Self-care principles

#### Module 4: Integration with Daily Life (4 lessons, 57 min)
1. **Mindful Sitting** (Video, 12 min)
   - Posture awareness
   - Desk work applications
   - Preventing tension buildup

2. **Walking with Awareness** (Exercise, 20 min)
   - Gait awareness
   - Natural movement patterns
   - Walking meditation integration

3. **Daily Check-in Practice** (Article, 15 min)
   - Creating a routine
   - Morning and evening practices
   - Journaling prompts

4. **Integration Assessment** (Quiz, 10 min)
   - Application questions
   - Planning your practice

#### Module 5: Advanced Techniques (4 lessons, 95 min)

   - Complex movement sequences

2. **Complex Movement Sequences** (Exercise, 35 min)

3. **Personal Practice Development** (Article, 20 min)
   - Creating your own practice

   - Comprehensive knowledge check

## Content Update Guide

### Updating Video URLs
```sql
UPDATE academy_lessons
SET content = jsonb_set(content, '{video_url}', '"https://your-cdn.com/video.mp4"')
WHERE content_type = 'video' AND id = 'lesson-uuid';
```

### Adding New Lessons
```sql
INSERT INTO academy_lessons (module_id, title, content_type, content, duration_minutes, order_index)
VALUES (
  'module-uuid',
  'New Lesson Title',
  'video',
  '{"video_url": "https://...", "transcript": "..."}',
  20,
  5  -- order in module
);
```

### Publishing Courses
```sql
UPDATE academy_courses
SET is_published = true
WHERE id = 'course-uuid';
```
